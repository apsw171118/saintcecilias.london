Our School

Saint Cecilia’s is a vibrant Church of England secondary school with a distinctive Christian ethos for pupils aged 11-18 years. The school first opened in September 2003 on the site of Wandsworth Boys School.

More info

Admissions

We welcome applications from all members of the community and ask all parents/carers and applicants to respect the Christian ethos of our school and recognise its importance to our community.

More info

Holistic Education

Saint Cecilia’s provides outstanding pastoral care. We seek to provide for the personal welfare of each individual child so that there are as few barriers to learning as possible.

more info

Sports

Saint Cecilia’s Church of England School enjoys an enviable reputation for producing highly successful individual and team performances in a wide range of sports.

more info

The Friends

The Friends of Saint Cecilia's is a registered charity formed by parents. They raise money to help support the school and bring the wider community together by organising social events throughout the year.
